Top ten things to do while quarantine (corona-time)

Author: Giri, Published on: 25 March 2020

Binge watch : Netflix, Prime and Hotstar

Watching movies and series is always fun. But when it comes to isolation or in these times of quarantine, it becomes a top to-do stuff. If you don't have an account, just create an account and get a 1-month trial period in netflix. I believe every streaming app gives some trial period. if you still don't want to use those, just stream some videos in youtube. Happy binge watching

Games: Play station or Mobile games

Are you sick not meeting your friends. You like games? Thanks to multiplayer interactive games like PUBG and other battle royal games which brings you close to reality, you can connect with your friends and sho(o/u)t around. If you are not a fan of blood and war, even a candy crush will help eat your time

3. Learn new stuffs: A Tech skill, pottery or DIY

In this internet age, learning some new skills become easy as never before. There are multiple blogs and vlogs to teach you "How to create/do stuffs".

4. Reading books

Reading books is not just a pass time hobby, it will surely improve the quality of life. Take out the books from your book shelves and start reading. If you don't have any in your kitty, you can read ebooks by downloading kindle or google play books

5. Re-visit albums

Old is good. Yeah, you can re-visit your old albums, ceremony recordings and you babies year-on-year growth photos. It will surely put a smile on your face.

6. Indoor games

If you really wanted out of digital gadgets, indoor games is one of very few options. Carrom, Cards and Business games can be played from two to four people. If you don't have the necessary game boards you just need a pen and paper to play games like tik-tok-toe.

7. Cook new dish

Cooking is a real stress buster. You can try to cook some new dishes with the available ingredients. P.S. Avoid cooking bats

8. Workout

You might hit some extra pounds staying in home for a week. Imagine what happens for a month. So make it a mandate. Spend atleast an hour every day, to reduce the lockdown fat growth.

9. Clean your home

It is not every one's cup of tea. But, some people do really like cleaning and organizing their homes. Even if you don't like it, you can do it for your well being and for this time being

10. Eat, Sleep, Repeat

Who knows, we might never get again such a leisure home time away from office, traffic and pollutions. After all, we work and earn hoping for a nice meal and a good night's sleep. So if you really don't like any of the above stuffs, without any hesitations you can "oOoO zZzZ oOzZ"....


Apart from the above ten, there are lot more interesting stuffs to do like Exploring new apps, writing some interesting content and forward in whatsapp and twitter, create some memes and funny mock videos. If you are with your family in this corona times, spend some quality time and open up some quality conversation with them. Even though this is a fearful times, we will never get this kinda together time. And sorry, going out and having fun is never an option. Take care and stay safe
